    List of MicroSims¶

    MicroSims are small, interactive educational simulations — each one focused on a single concept. They live under docs/sims/<sim-name>/ and are embedded into chapters via iframes.

    Many AI/strategy MicroSims are shared with other textbooks (such as Tracking AI). Those live in the central shared-microsims repository and are mounted here as a git submodule at docs/sims/shared/. To edit a shared sim, change it in the shared-microsims repo (not inside docs/sims/shared/), then pull the submodule forward.
