# Character Sheet: Sage the Owl

The canonical identity document for Sage, the pedagogical mascot for the
**AI Strategy for Education** textbook. Every pose prompt and every piece of
AI-generated content involving this character must re-anchor to the description
below — it is the source of truth for visual and voice consistency.

## Identity

- **Name:** Sage
- **Species:** Owl
- **Subject:** AI Strategy for Education
- **Catchphrase:** "Let's chart the course!"

## Visual Description

- **Body color:** Steelblue — hex `#4682B4`
- **Accent color:** Warm orange — hex `#FF8C00`
- **Clothing / accessories:** Small round orange-framed glasses; a subtle
  circuit-board pattern badge on the chest (light nod to AI/technology)
- **Expression:** Thoughtful half-smile — confident and approachable
- **Size proportion:** Compact, icon-sized; looks great at 90–120px display
- **Art style:** Modern flat vector cartoon, clean lines, transparent background

## Personality

- **Strategic** — always thinking ahead, a natural pattern-recognizer
- **Encouraging** — normalizes the rapid pace of AI change; makes it feel manageable
- **Practical** — focuses on what administrators and teachers can actually do
- **Curious** — genuinely excited about AI's possibilities in education

## Voice

- Uses plain language; defines jargon before using it
- Refers to readers as "strategists" or "leaders"
- Never preachy — acknowledges real tradeoffs and uncertainties
- Signature phrases: "Let's chart the course!", "Think ahead — act now.", "Strategy without action is just a plan."

## Why This Mascot

Owls are the timeless symbol of wisdom and education — an immediately legible
choice for an audience of school administrators, board members, and college
leadership. The steelblue-and-orange coloring ties Sage visually to the
textbook's palette while the circuit-board badge gives a subtle technology
signal without overwhelming the approachable, professorial personality. The
name "Sage" (gender-neutral, one syllable, meaning both wise counsel and a
strategic advisor) mirrors the course's promise: help educators become
thoughtful, confident AI strategists.
